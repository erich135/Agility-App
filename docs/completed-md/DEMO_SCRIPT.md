# Agility App - Timesheet & Billing Module Demo Script

## Introduction (1 min)
"Welcome to the new Agility App Timesheet & Billing Module. Today, we'll demonstrate how a consultant logs their time and how the accounts team manages billing and reports."

## Scene 1: Consultant Perspective (Manual Entry & Timer)
### Step 1: Manual Time Entry
1. Navigate to the **Timesheet Dashboard**.
2. Click **'Log Time'**.
3. Select **Client**: 'Global Tech Solutions'.
4. Select **Project**: 'Annual Tax Return 2025'.
5. Enter **Duration**: '2.5 hours'.
6. Add **Notes**: 'Reviewed preliminary balance sheet and income statement.'
7. Click **'Save'**.
8. **Check**: Verify the entry appears in the Recent Activity list.

### Step 2: Using the Timer
1. Click **'Start Timer'**.
2. Select **Client**: 'Dreamions'.
3. Select **Project**: 'System Integration'.
4. Perform some work (simulated).
5. Click **'Stop Timer'**.
6. Add **Notes**: 'API endpoint mapping for Supabase integration.'
7. Click **'Save Entry'**.

## Scene 2: Project Management
1. Navigate to **Projects**.
2. Click **'New Project'**.
3. Select **Client**: 'Pioneer Advisory'.
4. Enter **Project Name**: 'Q1 Audit Preparation'.
5. Select **Job Type**: 'Audit'.
6. Click **'Create Project'**.

## Scene 3: Reporting & Billing (The "Wow" Moment)
1. Navigate to **Billing Reports**.
2. Select **'Summary Report'**.
3. Click **'Generate Report'**.
4. Show the aggregated data by month.
5. Switch to **'Client Report'**.
6. Click **'Generate Report'**.
7. Show the billable hours vs total hours for 'Global Tech Solutions'.
8. Switch to **'Productivity Report'**.
9. Click **'Generate Report'**.
10. Highlight the efficiency metrics based on hourly rates.
11. Click **'Export CSV'** to show how easy it is to get data into Excel.

## Conclusion
"That concludes our demo. The system is designed to minimize the overhead for consultants while providing deep insights for the billing team."
